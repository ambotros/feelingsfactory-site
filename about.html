You're not too much.
You're human.

The Feelings Factory started as one t-shirt made after a therapy session. That first design — FAWN AF — came out of learning what "fawning" actually is: people-pleasing to an extreme, agreeing with everything, shrinking yourself to stay safe. Putting that word on a shirt turned out to be its own kind of therapy.

Every design since has come from the same place: real feelings that are usually easier to laugh about than talk about out loud. The overstimulation. The dismissive "at least you're alive." The days the earth feels like it's on fire and somehow that's fine. We make shirts for people who feel things fully and would rather wear the punchline than explain the whole story.
